Subscription questions

How much is Elite?

Elite is €15 per year — that's it. One simple yearly price for the full experience, no hidden fees.

Do I need an account to subscribe?

Yes — Elite is tied to your UniScout account so your matches and applications stay with you. If you're not signed in, the Go Elite button will take you to sign in first, then bring you back to checkout.

Is payment secure?

Payments are handled by Stripe, an industry-leading, PCI-compliant payment provider. UniScout never sees or stores your card details.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, from your account's billing portal. You keep Elite until the end of your billing period — no lock-in.
