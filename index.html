Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before you start.

What exactly is UniScout?

UniScout is an AI-powered platform that helps you discover, compare and apply to universities. You tell us about yourself once, and we score every university by how well it fits you, then help you build and submit a professional application package.

Is it really free?

Yes. You can explore every university, compare options, save favourites and track deadlines completely free. Advanced AI matching and the application assistant are part of the Elite plan — but you never need a card to get started.

How does the AI matcher work?

It reads your grades, budget, preferred subjects, languages and goals, then compares them against each university's real requirements and data to produce a personalised fit score — and shows you the reasoning behind every match.

Which universities and countries are covered?

UniScout covers leading universities across dozens of countries, with deep detail on programmes, tuition, languages, entry difficulty and student cities — and we're always adding more.

Can I cancel Elite anytime?

Absolutely. Elite is billed yearly and you can cancel whenever you like from your account — no lock-in, no hassle.
